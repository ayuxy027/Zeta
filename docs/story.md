# Zeta Origin Story

## The Beginning

In July 2025, while working at [Heizen](https://heizen.work) (formerly Open Gig), I was fascinated by their internal tool "PM Agent" through which they synced progress across their overall 100 employees worldwide and took standups on the same daily. I saw this as a great thing but noticed many flaws in it.

## The Journey Continues

In September 2025, after my exit from Heizen, I started working at [Anaqua](https://anaqua.com) where we built our internal tool this time, but it wasn't as efficient as I had hoped. This experience reinforced my belief that there was a significant pain point that needed addressing in the market.

## Vexa.ai Discovery

During our research, we came across [Vexa.ai](https://github.com/Vexa-ai/vexa), an open source meeting transcription tool, which gave our belief in the product a major boost.

## The Birth of Zeta

After doing R&D for 2 months, I started the ideation of Zeta in early August 2025. Sumeet and Swapnil joined while setting up the idea and they joined the mission. They were also fascinated by the productivity boost they saw while serving their freelance clients.

## Early Testing

We tested Zeta for the first time when we worked for hackathons. It helped us one-shot the PRDs and standout features and action points for them effortlessly.

## Beta Development

In November 2025, we started development of the beta version of Zeta. We are currently working on a beta preview release. In testing, we used a pilot version which was simpler and very much problem focused.

## The Vision

Now we have decided to scale Zeta to millions of users and would love to have you onboard.
